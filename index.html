#About

This game is endless runner where player is rotating around generated planets. Main goal of the game is to orbit as many times as you can avoiding asteroids!

In order to do that you have to change your orbit with UP and DOWN arrow keys.

You can destroy asteroids with force field by pressing SPACEBAR but you can do this only once per loop!
